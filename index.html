PCS Genesis Schedule Viewer

Doesn't work on mobile, for now!
  1. Drag this link into your bookmarks bar!
  2. Sign in to Genesis in a parent account
  3. Click the bookmark
  4. Enter your student ID
  5. Your schedule will appear!
Join our Discord if you're in '23-'25

Not working? Make sure you followed the steps correctly.

  • Doublecheck that you dragged the yellow link into your bookmarks bar, not the link to this page.
  • You should be looking at the Genesis tab when you click the bookmark, not any other tab.
  • Verify that you are logged in to a parent account, not a student account.
  • Some districts have discovered and disabled the method we use to get schedules. Check with your friends to see if it's still working in your district.
